Before you send — the questions we get most
How fast do you actually reply?

Within one UK business day, every time. Average over the last 30 days: ~4 hours. The exception is weekends — replies will land Monday morning.

Will you reply if I'm not your target client?

Yes — usually with a redirect to who can help, plus a paragraph on what your archetype's most common trap is. We say no often, and we say it kindly.

Do you take cold pitches from agencies / sales teams?

Honestly, no. They go to a no-reply folder. If you're a service provider with something genuinely complementary, write a real human note — those we read and sometimes act on.

What's the fastest way to actually engage you?

Take the free 15-minute assessment. The Revenue Intelligence Report it produces is the most useful 90 seconds of work we'll ever do for you for free, and it makes any subsequent call 10× more useful.

Do you do free discovery calls?

No. The free assessment IS our discovery — and it's better than any call because it's evidence-based, not vibes-based. Paid Strategy Sessions (£950 · 30 min) start where the assessment ends.

Where are you based?

London, United Kingdom. We work with operators across UK, US, and EU. Servers in Helsinki, Finland (EU). UK GDPR + DPA 2018 aligned.
